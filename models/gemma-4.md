# Gemma 4: Google's Most Capable Open Models

**Category:** Frontier / Models
**Key Concepts:** Open-Source Models, Mixture of Experts (MoE), Agentic Workflows

## TL;DR
Google has introduced Gemma 4, its most capable open models to date. Released under an Apache 2.0 license, the Gemma 4 family is built for advanced reasoning and agentic workflows. It is available in four sizes: Effective 2B (E2B), Effective 4B (E4B), 26B Mixture of Experts (MoE), and 31B Dense. The models deliver state-of-the-art intelligence-per-parameter, with the 31B and 26B versions ranking highly on industry leaderboards while running efficiently on local hardware.

---

## The Gemma 4 Lineup

Built from the same research and technology as Gemini 3, the Gemma 4 family provides a powerful complement of open tools. The models move beyond simple chat interfaces to handle complex logic and agentic workflows.

The lineup includes four versatile sizes:
*   **Effective 2B (E2B):** Redefines on-device utility, prioritizing multimodal capabilities and low-latency processing over raw parameter count. Ideal for edge devices.
*   **Effective 4B (E4B):** A step up in capability while maintaining a footprint small enough for seamless ecosystem integration on devices.
*   **26B Mixture of Experts (MoE):** Utilizing a [Mixture of Experts](../concepts/mixture-of-experts.md) architecture, this model delivers high performance with efficient compute usage, securing the #6 spot on the Arena AI text leaderboard and outcompeting much larger models.
*   **31B Dense:** The flagship open model of the family, delivering state-of-the-art performance for its size. It currently ranks #3 globally among open models on the Arena AI text leaderboard.

## Apache 2.0 License: Complete Digital Sovereignty

A crucial aspect of Gemma 4 is its release under the permissive Apache 2.0 license. Unlike many frontier models that have restrictive terms, this provides developers with complete flexibility and digital sovereignty. Users have total control over their data, infrastructure, and models, allowing them to build freely and deploy securely across any environment, whether on-premises or in the cloud, without needing an internet connection.

## Real-World Application & Who Should Care

🚀 **THE PERFORMANCE MONSTERS (SOTA Seekers):**
Researchers and advanced developers can leverage the 31B Dense and 26B MoE models to achieve frontier-level reasoning and agentic capabilities locally. The open-source nature allows for deep customization, fine-tuning, and integration into complex, secure environments without data leaving the premises.

💰 **THE COST & LATENCY OPTIMIZERS (API Developers):**
The E2B and E4B models are game-changers for high-volume, low-latency applications. By running these highly capable smaller models at the edge or on local infrastructure, teams can drastically cut down on expensive cloud API calls while maintaining rapid response times for users.

💻 **THE EVERYDAY PROMPT ENGINEERS:**
Everyday users and developers can run Gemma 4 locally on standard hardware, including some laptop GPUs. This means you can experiment with powerful agentic workflows and advanced reasoning privately and for free, without relying on paid subscriptions to proprietary models.
